Open Role

We are looking for a talented and enthusiastic individual to join our team as a Javascript Developer.

Software Development
Kigali, Rwanda
Deadline on Sat Jul 22 2023 12:00:00 GMT+0200 (Central Africa Time)

Job Description

We are looking for a talented and enthusiastic individual to join our team as IST Africa is a subsidiary of IST Group. We’re a leading EdTech company in Scandinavia. Our vision is to create the best opportunities for everyone to learn more. Together, we create the best conditions for learning – today and in the future. For decades we’ve been developing state of the art tech solutions that make education processes more efficient and effective for students, parents, teachers and school administrators. Our team is a very diverse team of 400+ coworkers with 9 different offices across Europe.

Now we’re opening our first office in Africa!

Requirements


In establishing our Africa office, starting with Rwanda, we're building a dynamic team of experienced, open minded and flexible developers who find it interesting to work with fellow developers across Europe, developing world class tech solutions that are to revolutionize the global education systems. If you confidently think you have deep domain expertise as a JavaScript Developer in the following technology stack, we’re definitely looking for you


  • JavaScript/TypeScript
  • React, React Native or Vue.js
  • Node.js
  • REST, GraphQL
  • SQL
  • Docker, JSON, Swagger

Benefits

Besides working for a global leading EdTech company with experienced and professional teams that make you the best you can be as a developer, our remunerations packages are much higher with a blend of other packages, using most recent devices and working in a serene and cozy environment (our Rwanda remote offices).

  • Competitive salary
  • Healthcare benefits
  • Flexible working hours
  • Opportunities for career growth
