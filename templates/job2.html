Job Description

We are seeking a skilled and motivated Network Engineer to join our dynamic IT team. As a Network Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, maintaining, and optimizing our organization's network infrastructure. Your role will involve collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure the reliable and efficient operation of our network systems. You will also be expected to troubleshoot and resolve any network-related issues that may arise.

Requirements


  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • React, React Native or Vue.js
  • Proven work experience as a Network Engineer or similar role.
  • Solid understanding of network protocols, technologies, and topologies (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, VLANs, VPN, etc.).
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Hands-on experience with network hardware devices (routers, switches, firewalls, load balancers, etc.).
  • Relevant certifications (e.g., CCNA, CCNP, JNCIA) are a plus.
