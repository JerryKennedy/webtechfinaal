Financial Analyst
Kigali, Rwanda
63 Applied to this job
Deadline on Sat Jul 22 2023 12:00:00 GMT+0200 (Central Africa Time)

Job Description

We are currently seeking a skilled and detail-oriented Financial Analyst to join our finance team. As a Financial Analyst, you will play a critical role in providing valuable insights and analysis to support strategic decision-making within our organization. You will work closely with various stakeholders to assess financial performance, identify trends, and make recommendations to drive business growth and profitability.

Now we’re opening our first office in Africa!

Requirements

Conduct financial analysis, including variance analysis, budgeting, forecasting, and financial modeling. Prepare and present financial reports, performance metrics, and KPIs to management and stakeholders. Collaborate with department heads to develop budgets and monitor spending, providing guidance to ensure adherence to financial targets. Perform in-depth analysis of financial data to identify trends, opportunities, and risks. Assist in the preparation of business cases and financial justifications for new projects or investments.


  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or a related field.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment and meet tight deadlines.
  • Proven work experience as a Financial Analyst or similar role.
  • Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel and other financial software applications.
  • Solid understanding of financial principles, accounting practices, and financial reporting standards
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills to convey complex financial information to non-financial stakeholders.

  • Career growth and advancement opportunities within the organization.
